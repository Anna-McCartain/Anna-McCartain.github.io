About Me

I am a second year student at the University of Manchester, studying Computer Science. In addition to my love for academia, I have an undeniable artistic side and produce music under the artist name Paper Swan. 

I believe music to be analogous to computer science; a language where mathematical reasoning is woven into the foundations, yet still an abundance of innovation and creative freedom prevails.
